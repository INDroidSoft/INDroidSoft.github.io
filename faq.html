Is account registration required?

Yes account registration is required befor you start playing. This ensures a valid communication channel for all parties involved in any transactions.

Redemption

What modes of payment do you support?

Currently we support PayTM and UPI.

How do i redeem my prize money?

After you finsih at the top of any leaderbaord at the end of the day i.e. 00:00 the prize money according to the prize pool will be added in your account. After that you can submit a request for redmption. The steps involved in this process are really simple. All you need to do is:
  • Tap Redeem.
  • Enter PayTM or UPI id.
  • Tap Submit Request.
  • The next step is the approval step, which usually takes about 12 hours. After that you will get cash in your preferred account.
Quiz

What languages are quizes available in?

Currently the quizes are available in English & Hindi.

What are the timing for quiz?

We have two categories: Daily Quiz & Mega Quiz. Daily Quiz runs all day from 00:00 to 23:59 Mega Quiz is held for a limited time usually 3 to 4 hours everyday

How are winners decided?

Winners are decided at the end of each day at 23:59.
Top 3 players in each leaderboards are declared as winners and prize money is shared according to prize pool between them.
